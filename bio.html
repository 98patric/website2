about patrick stephan


patrick stephan is a 25 year old saxophonist and private woodwind instructor based in heidelberg, germany.

he started his musical journey with the beginning of the fifth grade. since then patrick has been practicing, gigging and teaching everyday. music was always in the house when patrick was a child. he was deeply motivated to connecting deeper with music.

patrick has been giving music classes in schools, kindergardens and in private lessons as a freelancer for three years. his professional jazz education patrick gained from studying with alberto menendez. in addition, he had various performances as a bandleader with his trio and quartet on the alto saxophone.

patrick is searching for new sounds playing his jazz-oriened music and loves to enter undetected musical spheres.
